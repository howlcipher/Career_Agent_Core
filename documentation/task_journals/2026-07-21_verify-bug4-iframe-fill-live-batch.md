- 2026-07-21 ~18:35 ‚Äî **Root cause found**: `ps aux` revealed five separate `/tmp/go-build*/b001/exe/main` processes still alive, one from *every* kill-and-relaunch cycle performed tonight (10:15, 13:15, 14:20, 15:07, 17:37). `go run` doesn't exec into its compiled binary ‚Äî it stays alive as a wrapper around a separately-spawned child process.
